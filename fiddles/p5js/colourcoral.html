Coral Colour Picker (more below!)

Welcome to the fractal coral colour picker! This is a challenging colour picker made by caitelatte in 2017 as part of an assignment for Art & Interaction in New Media, a course at the Australian National University.

Click anywhere on the canvas to pick a colour to start from. The middle floating fractal will start from the selected colour each time it is changed. As the circles that make up each fractal move further from the centre, they also move closer to red, green or blue. This should make it possible to generate most colours that you can think of, given enough persistence and luck!

Settings for your Colour Picker
