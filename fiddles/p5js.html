p5js fiddles!

Here are some p5js generative fiddles/sketches that I've made!

  • Diamond Game: a little calming platform game where you race to collect the diamonds!
  • Octagonal: a demonstration of a tesselating octagon-square pattern.
    • Nametag: a nametag generator using the octagonal pattern-maker. Press space to capture a frame and download a name tag!
  • Colour Coral: a fractal-based colour picker which looks like a coral and has a sneaky environmental message!
  • Blooming Garden: a lovely sound flower garden where you can click and add more flowers. Might be a lil loud.
  • Kaleiding: a kaleidoscopey mix of colours, symmetry and transparency. Fiddle with the variables!
