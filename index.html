hi there! this is caitlin maclatte's website.

She can be found on twitter, github and in Canberra, Australia at the Australian National University studying undergraduate computer science. If you like security, Cait also has keybase!

what does cait do?

Cait does adviceformerrin.tumblr.com and some of the posts on catviceforben.tumblr.com.

She's been very involved with the ANU Computer Science Students' Association as a General Representative on the committee since September 2013.

Some interesting projects Cait has worked on include:

  • caitessegonal, a plugin written in C++ for video editing software Nuke which is meant to render a tesselating pattern of octagons and squares. It was made for a digital art course in semester one of 2014. You can check out its development over at caitelartv2619.tumblr.com
  • caiteshell, a very basic shell program written in C for a programming course in semester one of 2014. It doesn't produce any seg faults that she knows of, which is something to be proud of.

Cait is very interested in how easy it is to work her name into the names of her projects and also delicious food.
